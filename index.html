ABOUT

Omotayo is an aspiring designer with a multidisciplinary field in illustration, branding, UI/UX and more. Growing up in London, being exposed to various groups and customs has influenced the methodologies and conceptual outcomes Tayo creates in a positive, uplifting way.

Tayo's hobbies include gaming, cooking, media and language learning, all of which have also contributed to the character and personality in their work.
